> awakening = done

After realization, you are often:

a newborn adult

with a rebooted nervous system

and very few skills for how to operate it


Except:

> “Don’t believe your old thoughts anymore.”



Helpful… but not a life plan.

This is why so many awakened people:

wander

lose relationships

lose pleasure

lose meaning

melt down

become convinced they “broke reality”


Not because they saw truth.
Because no one gave them the user manual for the human that remains.

These aren’t spiritual extras.
